About Me

- Eric Putko -
Web Developer & Creative Explorer

I am a recent graduate with an Associate of Science degree in Computer Information Systems (CIS) specializing in Web Development from Cuyamaca College, where I honed a strong foundation in both technical and creative aspects of the field. My proficiency spans front-end and back-end technologies—including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL—as well as graphic design and database management. With a Certificate of Specialization in Web Programming and ongoing certifications in CompTIA A+ and Java programming, I remain committed to continuous learning and staying adaptable in today’s rapidly evolving tech industry.

In every role I’ve undertaken, I have demonstrated a keen attention to detail, a passion for problem-solving, and a drive to optimize processes. I thrive in collaborative environments, valuing diverse perspectives and open communication to achieve project goals that resonate with users. Whether contributing as a front-end, back-end, or full-stack developer, I bring a dynamic and balanced skill set to every team endeavor.

Outside of work, my adventurous spirit fuels my creativity. I find inspiration in the great outdoors—whether I’m hiking scenic trails, capturing moments through photography, or experiencing the thrill of motorcycle riding. On a personal note, I’m excited to begin a new chapter as I prepare to marry my fiancée of nine incredible years this June, a milestone that reflects my commitment to growth both personally and professionally.

I am eager to leverage my versatile technical skills and creative mindset to build impactful web experiences and contribute to innovative projects in the tech industry.
