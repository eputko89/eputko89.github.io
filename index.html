About Me

- Eric Putko -
Web Developer & Creative Explorer

Hi, I’m Eric Putko, a web developer with a passion for building meaningful digital experiences and a background in management, accounting, and administration. After seven years in diverse professional roles, I decided to pursue my long-term interest in technology and completed my Associate degree in Computer Information Systems with a focus on Web Development.

I’ve since applied my skills in real-world projects, including a nonprofit internship where I built and maintained a WordPress site with WooCommerce and Square POS integration to support volunteer scheduling, client shopping, and donation workflows. Alongside HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, I’ve also gained experience with Java programming and enjoy blending technical precision with creative problem-solving.

Outside of work, I’m a photographer who loves capturing nature, landscapes, and astrophotography. I bring that same creativity and attention to detail into my web projects — focusing on clean design, accessibility, and user-friendly experiences.

I’m excited to grow in a professional development role where I can contribute my skills, learn from a team, and continue crafting solutions that have a real impact.
